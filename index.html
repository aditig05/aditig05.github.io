Speculative Refinement: A Hybrid Autoregressive Diffusion Decoding Strategy and Its Behavior Across Benchmarks

Aditi Gupta*, Neel Mishra, Kushagra Trivedi, Pawan Kumar

ACL 2026 · GEM Workshop

We propose a training-free hybrid decoding framework that combines autoregressive generation with masked diffusion refinement. The method uses entropy-guided selective masking — refining the top 60% highest-entropy tokens from an AR draft using a masked diffusion model — improving absolute code-generation accuracy by up to 25.8 points across HumanEval and MBPP, while reducing GSM8K decoding latency by 2.43×.

Hierarchical Sparse Plus Low-Rank Compression of LLM

Aditi Gupta*, Pawan Kumar

CODS 2025 · 13th ACM IKDD Conference on Data Science

We develop a rank-structured compression pipeline for LLM weight matrices using sparse outlier separation, RCM reordering, and recursive HSS factorization. The method achieves 1.7× memory reduction on LLaMA-7B self-attention projections (1.6B parameters) while maintaining WikiText perplexity of 1.64 — outperforming dense baselines (5.83) and sparse+SVD approaches.
